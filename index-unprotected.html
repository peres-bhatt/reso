Historical - Overview:




The surname Peres Bhatt is believed to have originated from the Brahmin name Bhatt which is of Indian origin and is derived from the Sanskrit word bhaṭṭa, which means “lord” and is based on bhartr-, which means “lord husband” and is a derivative of bhar-, which means “to bear support.”




As per the legend, some members of the priestly class in the Konkana community were converted to Catholicism by Portuguese missionaries, probably in the 18th century. Their godfathers were the Dukes of Peres, whose surnames were adopted after conversion.
