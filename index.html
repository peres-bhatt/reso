Let's Discover our Roots

Brief History:

The surname Peres Bhatt was originally Bhat - the priestly class in the Konkana community. According to legend, some members of this priestly class were converted to Catholicism by Portuguese missionaries - probably in the 18th century.Their godfathers were the Dukes of Peres, whose surnames has been adopted after conversion.
